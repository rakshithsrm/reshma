✦ A Letter Under the Stars ✦

For My Sister

Written with love · On your birthday

My dearest Sister,

Tonight, I looked up at the sky and counted every star I could find — and still, not one of them is as bright as you have been in my life. On this special day, I want you to know that having you as my sister is the greatest gift this universe ever gave me.

Do you remember all those nights when the world felt too heavy, and you were always the first one there — with a warm word, a quiet hug, or just your presence that made everything feel a little more okay? That is who you are. You carry light in you, Sister, even when you don't see it yourself.

You have taught me what it means to be strong with grace, to love without conditions, and to laugh even in the hardest moments. Every lesson you gave me — some in words, most just by being you — has shaped who I am today. I owe so much of myself to you.

On this birthday, I wish you skies full of possibilities, mornings that feel like gifts, and evenings as beautiful as this one. I wish you rest when you need it, adventure when you crave it, and love in every corner of every day you live.

You deserve every good thing this world has — and so much more than the world even knows how to give. So tonight, under these stars, I am sending every wish I have straight up into the sky — all of them, for you.

Keep shining, Sister. The stars have nothing on you. 🌟

With all the love in my heart,
— Rakshith 💛
